Our Mission

The specialty coffee industry has seen significant growth over the past decade which has invited a large wave of small coffee roasters. However, the barrier for entry to coffee roasting is quite steep, as the amount of training and overhead required for roasting is intensive. CupChoice aims to simplify the coffee roasting process, allowing customers to customize the roast to their preferred flavor profile using an intuitive and cost-effective platform.

CupChoice has developed a roast monitoring software for collecting and cataloging real-time temperature data of coffee roasts, providing insights and recommendations, and connecting roasters with coffee producers globally. As of 2023, the specialty coffee roasting market size is $4 billion and growing. New roasting ventures will waste on average 5000 pounds of coffee every year due to bad batches produced by errors in their roasting process. CupChoice works to reduce the waste produced and save on lost revenue by preventing frequent errors when roasting.

Due to the steep learning curve of roasting, on average it takes a business 3+ years to break even on their investments due to poor planning. Our software alleviates initial pressure through green coffee sourcing, personalized roasting profiles, and an unmatched user interface, surpassing competitors. CupChoice is actively exploring a niche market where no existing product comprehensively caters to the needs of small roasters. Promoting the product at the three largest coffee events in North America, CupChoice aims to sell over 5,300 subscriptions within the first two years.

Our team of founders have an extensive background in engineering and development with a deep passion for coffee roasting. CupChoice aims to revolutionize your approach to coffee crafting to ensure roasters receive consistent desired results. Empower your roasting venture with CupChoice and revolutionize your approach to coffee crafting.
